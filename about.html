About Us

Welcome to SamThing Baked, where passion meets the oven! What started as a simple hobby quickly grew into a beloved brand that has captured the hearts of our customers. Established in 2015, SamThing Baked began as an online business, offering customized cakes and pastries tailored to individual tastes and preferences.

Our journey began with two professionals from different fields—a statistician and a businessman in the IT industry—who decided to take a leap of faith and turn their shared love for baking into a full-time venture. This dream soon blossomed into something much bigger, and in June 2024, we proudly opened our first physical store in Bulacan. Since then, we have continued to grow, bringing our freshly baked goods to more communities with new locations at Adamson University (November 2024) and Mapúa University-Makati (February 2025). We remain committed to delivering quality pastries and cakes that bring joy to every occasion.

As we continue to grow, we remain dedicated to innovation and excellence in baking. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has allowed us to introduce new product lines, including healthier pastry options and customizable treats for special occasions. Each recipe is crafted with precision and passion, ensuring that every bite brings warmth and happiness.

Looking ahead, we are excited to bring our artisanal baked goods to more locations and expand our community of loyal customers. Whether you’re celebrating milestones or simply indulging in a sweet treat, SamThing Baked is here to make every moment more special.

At SamThing Baked, we believe in creating not just delicious treats but memorable experiences for every customer. Our products are crafted with the finest ingredients, made from the heart, and delivered with care. Whether you’re grabbing a quick snack between classes or treating yourself to something sweet, we’ve got something for everyone.

Thank you for being part of our journey—we couldn’t have come this far without your support!
